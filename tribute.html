Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Scientist, Teacher & Visionary Leader

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was one of the greatest scientists and the 11th President of India. He was known for his simplicity, dedication, and love for education.

Early Life

Dr. Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He came from a humble background and worked very hard to achieve his dreams.

Achievements

  • Known as the Missile Man of India
  • Played a key role in India's space and missile programs
  • Served as the 11th President of India (2002–2007)
  • Awarded Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award

Why I Admire Him

I admire Dr. Kalam because of his positive attitude, simplicity, and dedication towards education. He always motivated students to dream big and work hard to achieve their goals.

“Excellence is a continuous process, not an accident.”
